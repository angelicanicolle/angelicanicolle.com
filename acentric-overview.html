ACENTRIC MAGAZINE | OVERVIEW

Acentric Magazine is an online and print magazine covering a variety of topics from music, lifestyle, local culture, and everything in between.

As the editor-in-chief of the publication, I oversee the creation of all content web and print content; lead a team of talented writers, photographers, designers and illustrators; and design Acentric Magazine's quarterly issues.

Primary Roles:

  • Editor-in-Chief
  • Creative Director
  • Lead Designer
