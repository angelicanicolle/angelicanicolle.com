Kizmet

UI Mobile Application Design

Kizmet is an application designed to make face-to-face networking easier for the working professional.

Too often, networking becomes too time consuming, awkward or inconvenient. However, the truth still stands that networking events bring valuable connections.

Kizmet takes the work out of networking as it was created in order to create convenient, comfortable and efficient networking experiences. Aimed towards users who are app savvy, mobile oriented and social, Kizmet offers a mobile based application that connects working professionals face-to-face in group settings, allows recurring scheduling and on-demand meetups for avid networkers, and works well in conference environments.
